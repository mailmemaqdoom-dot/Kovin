The following prompt pack is designed to keep all 10 Kovin visuals within one consistent premium campaign universe. The intended tone is premium consumer electronics, inspired by world-class product campaign language rather than generic e-commerce banners.

## Recommended Generation Settings

| Setting | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| Aspect ratio | **21:9** for hero banners and campaign visuals; **16:9** if the platform does not support ultra-wide output |
| Composition safety | Keep the main subject in the central 60 percent of the frame and preserve clean negative space on at least one side for headline overlay |
| Style strength | Medium to high realism; avoid illustrative or painterly modes unless specifically required |
| Text handling | Do not ask the model to render detailed text. Add Kovin identity as subtle color, signage, silhouette, or abstract monogram presence instead |
| Post-production direction | Premium contrast, warm highlights, soft shadow depth, realistic reflections, controlled depth of field |
